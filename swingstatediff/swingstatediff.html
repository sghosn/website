Swing State Result compiler and comparison

Different websites compile and upload results at different times. If you're trying to retrieve real time info, you want the fastest website. This compiles and compares website.

HTML, CSS, Python: Flask, Javascript: Jquery

Why implement?

There are three different results aggregators. DecisionDeskHQ, Edison, and the AP. They upload results and compile them at different times. On nights like election night, which are intensely busy, having a way of seeing which is fastest is crucial when you're attempting to find which source is most accurate and fast.

Implementation

This project used jQuery to obtain the information and then Flask, JavaScript, CSS, and HTML to display the information dynamically.
