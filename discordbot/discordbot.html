COVID-19 Visualization

During the beginning of the pandemic, New York City was hit extremely hard by COVID-19. This is a visualization I conducted during its April peak in NYC.

This chart shows positivity rates by zipcodes in the New York City region.

You can zoom in to the specific zip codes.

This graph shows new and cumulative hospitalized, total case, and death counts.

This bar graph shows the differences in death, hospitalization, and covid counts by age group.

This bar graph shows the differences in death, hospitalization, and covid counts by gender.
